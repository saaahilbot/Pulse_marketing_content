- Employee Engagement Survey Template
- For HR teams and company leadership, employee engagement might be the ultimate barometer for success.
- Employee engagement software helps organizations measure and improve workplace satisfaction, morale, and commitment. It enables HR teams to collect feedback, analyze sentiment, and act on insights to create a more engaged and productive workforce.
- How does Lattice’s employee engagement software empower HR teams?
- Lattice's platform empowers HR teams by offering a centralized system for engagement, performance management, feedback, and continuous improvement. Features like real-time analytics and personalized insights enable HR professionals to proactively address employee needs and enhance overall workplace satisfaction.
- Lattice supports pulse surveys, eNPS, onboarding, offboarding, and exit surveys. HR teams can also create custom surveys to address specific needs, such as retention analysis or post-event feedback​​.
- How does Lattice help organizations analyze engagement survey results?
- Lattice provides advanced analytics tools, including heatmaps, sentiment tracking, and trend comparisons. These insights allow organizations to filter results by demographics, departments, or custom fields to pinpoint engagement gaps​​.
- How does Lattice handle action planning post-survey?
- of articles, templates, and webinars to assist organizations in developing and executing effective engagement strategies. These resources cover topics such as building a feedback culture, conducting performance reviews, and improving company culture.
- How does Lattice use sentiment analysis with employee engagement data?
- Sentiment analysis in Lattice interprets employee survey comments to identify positive, neutral, and negative trends. This feature helps HR teams prioritize areas of concern and take proactive action​​.
- Lattice enables organizations to filter engagement data by diversity metrics, helping HR teams assess how inclusive and equitable their workplace is. This supports the creation of actionable DEIB strategies​​.
- Lattice's employee engagement solutions include pulse surveys, onboarding and exit surveys, eNPS measurement, and engagement benchmarking powered by Mercer data. AI-powered analysis surfaces key drivers and recommended actions from survey results, helping HR teams understand not just what employees feel but why, and what to do about it.
