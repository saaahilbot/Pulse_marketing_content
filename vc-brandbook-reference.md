## Colors

### Core Palette

| Name | HEX | RGB | Role |
|------|-----|-----|------|
| Space Cadet | `#29294C` | 41, 41, 76 | Primary brand — trust, professionalism, stability |
| Pumpkin Orange | `#FF6D05` | 255, 109, 5 | Accent / CTA — enthusiasm, creativity, energy |
| Ghost White | `#EEEEF6` | 238, 238, 246 | Background — majority of design space |
| Lavender Web | `#CDCDE4` | 205, 205, 228 | Padding between content sections, grouping |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | 255, 255, 255 | Default base |

### Color Philosophy
- **Space Cadet** = foundation. Headers, body text, UI elements. Symbolises trust and credibility.
- **Pumpkin Orange** = action. CTAs, highlights, key messages only. Never as a large background.
- **Ghost White** = breathing room. Predominates all layouts for clarity.
- **Lavender Web** = grouping. Use specifically for padding between content boxes — not as a primary background.

### Space Cadet Tint Scale (dark → light)
`#29294C` → `#31315A` → `#3F3F74` → `#505095` → `#6A6AAF` → `#8B8BC0` → `#ACACD2` → `#CDCDE4` → `#EEEEF6`

### Pumpkin Orange Tint Scale (dark → light)
`#FF6D05` → `#FF7E39` → `#FF8E55` → `#FF9D6C` → `#FFAC83` → `#FFC8AD` → `#FFD6C1` → `#FFE4D6`

## Logo

- Two-part: **Graphic Mark** (flower with hand-shaped petals = teamwork, inclusivity) + **Word Mark** (VANTAGECIRCLE in Space Cadet)
- Always use together — never split
- **Positive variant** on light backgrounds; **Negative variant** on dark backgrounds
- Never skew, rotate, add effects, recolor, or separate the two marks

---

## Design Elements — Rounded Forms

Smooth, rounded shapes are the **cornerstone** of the visual identity. They convey harmony, accessibility, inclusivity, approachability, modernity, and positive engagement.

### Shape Variants and What Goes In Them
| Shape | Use for |
|-------|---------|
| Full Sphere | Imagery or design balance |
| Half Sphere | Imagery or design balance |
| Arc | Imagery or focused content |
| Stroke Sphere | Decorative / structural accent |
| Rounded Boxes | Focused content or design balance |

**Corner radii for rounded boxes:** 8px · 12px · 16px · 21px

All shapes use the core palette: Space Cadet, Pumpkin Orange, Lavender Web, Ghost White.

## Iconography

Two distinct styles — choose by size context:

**Small icons (inline / pointer):**
- Deep blue circle background: `#3C3C69`
- White icon centered inside with ample padding

**Large icons (feature highlights):**
- Multi-part icon
- Parts use: `#29294C` (Space Cadet), `#FF6D05` (Orange), `#CDCDE4` (Lavender Web)

## CTA Buttons

**Primary Button**
- Shape: capsule (pill)
- Height: 54px · Text: 18px white
- Fill: `#FF6D05`

**Ghost Button (secondary)**
- Shape: capsule outline
- Height: 54px · Text: 18px `#FF6D05`
- Border: `#FF6D05` · Background: transparent

**CTA Banners**
- Background: Space Cadet `#29294C`
- Use ghost button style inside
- Never use Pumpkin Orange as the banner background (orange reserved for button fills only)
- Adequate whitespace around CTAs at all times

## Photography

- Real humans, diverse backgrounds, genuine expressions
- Professional workplace: recognition moments, teamwork, collaboration
- Emphasise the humanity in employer–employee relationships
- **Not** gym, wellness, or fitness imagery — that belongs to Vantage Fit

## VFit vs VC — Keep Separate

| | Vantage Circle | Vantage Fit |
|--|----------------|-------------|
| Primary | Space Cadet `#29294C` | Fit Red `#F15162` |
| Accent | Pumpkin Orange `#FF6D05` | Aqua Mint `#41D8B4` |
