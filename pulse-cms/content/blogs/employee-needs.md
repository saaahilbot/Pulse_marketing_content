## Overview

This article covers everything HR leaders need to know about employee needs: what it means, how to measure it, how to interpret the data, and how to act on findings.

## Key Metrics to Track

- Engagement Index score (target: ≥70 = Strong)
- eNPS trend over time
- Category score vs. company benchmark
- Participation rate by department

## Acting on Survey Data

Once you have data, the action planning cycle matters most:

1. Identify departments scoring below 60
2. Read open-ended comments for qualitative context
3. Build specific action items with owners and deadlines
4. Resurvey in the next cycle to verify change
