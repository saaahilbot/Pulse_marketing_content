# Employee Survey Questions About Work Environment

The work environment covers everything from physical space to the psychological conditions that let employees do their best work. These questions surface issues before they become attrition drivers.

## Physical Workspace (In-Office)
1. My workspace is comfortable and well-equipped for my role.
2. The office environment is free from excessive noise and distractions.
3. I have adequate space to do my work effectively.
4. The office temperature and lighting are appropriate for focused work.
5. Meeting rooms and collaboration spaces are available when I need them.
6. I feel physically safe in my workplace.
7. The office facilities (kitchen, restrooms, quiet areas) meet my needs.

## Remote / Home Office Setup
8. I have a comfortable and productive home workspace.
9. I have the technology and equipment I need to work from home effectively.
10. My internet connection is reliable enough for remote work requirements.
11. I feel equally productive working remotely as I do in the office.
12. The company provides adequate support for my home office setup.

## Digital Work Environment
13. The tools and software I use help me do my job efficiently.
14. I can access the information and resources I need without unnecessary friction.
15. Technical issues are resolved quickly when they arise.
16. Communication tools make it easy to stay connected with my team.
17. Our digital systems and processes reduce my workload, not add to it.

## Psychological Safety & Inclusion
18. I feel comfortable expressing my opinions at work.
19. I feel included and respected by my colleagues.
20. People of all backgrounds are treated equitably in our workplace.
21. I can bring my authentic self to work.
22. I feel safe to take risks and try new approaches without fear of blame.
