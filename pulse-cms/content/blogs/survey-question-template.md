# Employee Survey Question Templates

Use these validated templates as-is or adapt them for your organisation. Each template is categorised by topic with recommended question types.

## Core Engagement Template (5 questions, ~3 min)

1. On a scale of 1–5, how motivated do you feel to do your best work right now?
2. I clearly understand how my work contributes to the company's goals. (1–5 agreement scale)
3. My manager provides me with the support I need to succeed. (1–5)
4. I feel valued and recognised for my contributions. (1–5)
5. What is the one thing that would most improve your day-to-day work experience? (Open-ended)

## eNPS Template (1 question)

1. On a scale of 0–10, how likely are you to recommend [Company] as a place to work?
   *(Follow-up, optional):* What is the main reason for your score? (Open-ended)

## Manager Effectiveness Template (6 questions)

1. My manager clearly communicates expectations. (1–5)
2. My manager gives me useful feedback on my work. (1–5)
3. I feel comfortable bringing concerns to my manager. (1–5)
4. My manager supports my professional development. (1–5)
5. My manager treats all team members fairly. (1–5)
6. What could your manager do differently to better support you? (Open-ended)

## Wellness & Wellbeing Template (5 questions)

1. I am able to maintain a healthy work-life balance. (1–5)
2. I feel my workload is manageable. (1–5)
3. I feel supported if I need to take time for my mental health. (1–5)
4. My physical work environment supports my wellbeing. (1–5)
5. What could the company do to better support your wellbeing? (Open-ended)

## Return-to-Office / Hybrid Template (4 questions)

1. The current office attendance policy works well for my role. (1–5)
2. I have the tools and equipment I need to be productive at home. (1–5)
3. The office environment supports collaboration when I'm on-site. (1–5)
4. What would make our hybrid work policy better for you? (Open-ended)
