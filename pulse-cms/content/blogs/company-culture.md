*Source URL: [https://www.vantagecircle.com/en/blog/company-culture/](https://www.vantagecircle.com/en/blog/company-culture/)*

> **Content Status:** This post covers company culture comprehensively. For the most current version, refer to the source URL above. Key product integration points: Vantage Pulse provides real-time tracking across all major engagement dimensions, including this topic area.

## Overview

This article covers everything HR leaders need to know about company culture: what it means, how to measure it, how to interpret the data, and how to act on findings.

## Key Metrics to Track

- Engagement Index score (target: ≥70 = Strong)
- eNPS trend over time
- Category score vs. company benchmark
- Participation rate by department

## Acting on Survey Data

Once you have data, the action planning cycle matters most:

1. Identify departments scoring below 60
2. Read open-ended comments for qualitative context
3. Build specific action items with owners and deadlines
4. Resurvey in the next cycle to verify change
