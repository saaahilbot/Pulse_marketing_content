# 30 Employee Pulse Survey Questions for Real-Time Insights

Pulse surveys work because they're short. The goal is 3–8 questions per check-in, sent frequently enough to catch sentiment shifts as they happen — not months after.

These 30 questions are designed for rotation across pulse cycles, keeping surveys fresh while tracking the same underlying dimensions over time.

## Engagement & Motivation (6 questions)
1. On a scale of 1–10, how engaged do you feel at work this week?
2. I feel motivated to go above and beyond in my role right now.
3. My energy levels at work have been mostly positive this week.
4. I feel proud to work for this company.
5. I feel connected to the mission of this organisation.
6. I am excited about what I'm working on right now.

## Manager & Team (6 questions)
7. My manager has checked in with me meaningfully this week/month.
8. I feel supported by my team.
9. Communication within my team has been clear.
10. I feel comfortable raising concerns with my manager.
11. My team is working well together toward shared goals.
12. I received useful feedback from my manager recently.

## Work Experience (6 questions)
13. My workload has been manageable.
14. I have had what I need to do my job well.
15. I feel my time at work has been well spent.
16. My day-to-day tasks feel meaningful.
17. I feel productive and focused in my current work setup.
18. There are few unnecessary obstacles slowing me down.

## Wellbeing (6 questions)
19. I have been able to maintain a healthy work-life balance.
20. I feel my stress levels are manageable.
21. I have taken proper breaks and downtime.
22. I feel physically well enough to do my best work.
23. I feel the company cares about my wellbeing.
24. I am not at risk of burnout in the near term.

## Quick Single-Question Pulses
25. How would you describe your overall mood at work this week? (1–5 emoji scale)
26. Would you recommend joining your team to a friend? (Yes / Probably / No)
27. What is one thing that would improve your workday right now? (Open-ended)
28. Do you feel your voice is heard in this organisation? (Yes / Sometimes / No)
29. How clear do you feel about your priorities for the next 30 days? (1–5)
30. eNPS: How likely are you to recommend this company as a place to work? (0–10)
