# 50 Employee Engagement Survey Questions That Get Honest Answers

Good survey questions don't lead employees to a desired answer — they create space for the truth. Here are 50 validated questions across the 10 core engagement categories measured in Vantage Pulse.

## Alignment (5 questions)
1. I understand how my work contributes to the company's overall goals.
2. The company's direction and priorities are clearly communicated to me.
3. My personal values align with the company's values.
4. Leadership makes decisions that are consistent with our stated values.
5. I believe the company is heading in the right direction.

## Autonomy (5 questions)
6. I have the freedom to decide how best to do my work.
7. I am trusted to make decisions within my area of responsibility.
8. I feel empowered to share ideas without fear of being dismissed.
9. My manager gives me space to work independently.
10. I can take ownership of problems without needing constant approval.

## Recognition (5 questions)
11. I feel recognised for the quality of work I do.
12. My contributions are acknowledged by my team and manager.
13. The recognition I receive reflects what I actually value.
14. Recognition at this company is given fairly and consistently.
15. I feel motivated by how my achievements are celebrated here.

## Relationship with Manager (5 questions)
16. My manager provides clear and helpful feedback.
17. I can approach my manager with problems or concerns.
18. My manager genuinely cares about my career development.
19. My manager treats all team members fairly.
20. I feel supported by my direct manager.

## Relationship with Peers (5 questions)
21. I enjoy working with my immediate team.
22. My team collaborates effectively to achieve shared goals.
23. I feel a sense of belonging within my team.
24. Conflicts in my team are resolved constructively.
25. I trust the people I work with directly.

## Wellness (5 questions)
26. I am able to maintain a healthy work-life balance.
27. My workload feels manageable on most days.
28. The company supports my mental health and wellbeing.
29. I feel physically safe in my work environment.
30. I rarely feel burned out at work.

## Personal Growth (5 questions)
31. I have opportunities to learn and grow in my role.
32. My manager actively supports my professional development.
33. I have a clear path for advancement at this company.
34. The company invests in training and skills development.
35. I am challenged in ways that help me grow.

## Communication (5 questions)
36. I receive the information I need to do my job well.
37. Senior leadership communicates transparently about the company's direction.
38. Important decisions are communicated to me in a timely way.
39. I know where to go when I have questions or concerns.
40. Feedback flows both ways — I can share as well as receive it.

## Work Environment (5 questions)
41. My physical or virtual workspace helps me be productive.
42. I have the tools and technology I need to do my job well.
43. The company's policies support a positive work environment.
44. I feel comfortable expressing myself at work.
45. The workplace is inclusive and respectful.

## Compensation & Benefits (5 questions)
46. I feel fairly compensated for the work I do.
47. The benefits offered by the company meet my needs.
48. I understand how compensation decisions are made here.
49. My total compensation is competitive with the market.
50. I feel the company values my financial wellbeing.
