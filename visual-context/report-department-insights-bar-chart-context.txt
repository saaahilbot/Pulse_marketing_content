1. CORE DATA / STAT SHOWN

Title: "Department Insights"
Twelve departments plotted against Engagement Score, e_NPS, and Participants Percentage:
App, Brand Sales, Corporate Sales, Design, Development, Finance, Human Resource, Marketing, Operations and Support, People & Culture, Quality Assurance, Research and Development.

Notable observations:
- Brand Sales has the highest Participants Percentage (~95%) — the top performer in engagement.
- Human Resource stands out with the highest Participants Percentage (~91%) as well as a high eNPS.
- Research and Development has the lowest Engagement Score (~67) and very low Participation (~20%), flagging a critical concern.
- Engagement Scores are relatively compressed in the 65–87 range across most departments, indicating a generally engaged workforce with pockets of risk.
