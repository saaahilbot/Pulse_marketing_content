1. CORE DATA / STAT SHOWN

Headline stat (bottom right): Gallup estimates that actively disengaged employees cost the U.S. between $483 billion and $605 billion each year in lost productivity.

The flow diagram illustrates a three-stage silent disengagement arc:
1. "You've built programs. You've launched surveys." (foundation — clipboard icon)
2. "But employees still leave without warning, quietly, and often, preventably." (warning — exit icon)
3. "And when they go, so does productivity, morale, and momentum." (consequence — declining chart icon)

Pull quote at bottom left: "You can't fix what you don't hear in time."

Dramatic and editorial. Dark navy/charcoal background with white text and soft circle icons, creating a moody, high-contrast atmosphere. The three-stage stacked flow reads bottom-to-top, building tension. A greyscale photo of a woman carrying a box (leaving the office) occupies the right half, making the emotional stakes visceral. The Gallup citation at the bottom right adds hard credibility. This is storytelling-as-data — cinematic rather than clinical.
