1. CORE DATA / STAT SHOWN

Title: "AccessOne Strengths"

Vantage Pulse's segment analysis revealed AccessOne's three highest-scoring categories:
1. Relationship with Peers (top — pink/coral blob)
2. Work Environment (right — orange blob)
3. Relationship with Managers (bottom — teal/blue blob)

These three categories scored the highest across AccessOne's entire survey, identifying them as the organisation's cultural strengths. The three segments are arranged in a loose circular cluster, each labelled with a callout line pointing to the relevant blob shape.

2. VISUAL VIBE

Organic and distinctive. Rather than a standard chart, this uses abstract blob/amoeba shapes in brand colours (pink, orange, teal) arranged in a cluster — the "AccessOne Strengths" label sits in the white space at the centre. Dark background continues the premium case study aesthetic. The organic shapes deliberately avoid the rigidity of a pie chart or Venn diagram, suggesting that strengths are fluid and interconnected rather than siloed. This is one of the most visually unique charts in the entire collateral library.
