1. CORE DATA / STAT SHOWN

Title: "Demographic Insights by Gender"
Three demographic groups (Female, Male, Undisclosed) each measured across three KPIs:
- Engagement Score (dark grey bar)
- Participants Percentage (orange bar)
- e_NPS (light blue bar)

Key findings visible in the chart:
- Female employees: Engagement ~75, Participation ~81, eNPS ~57
- Male employees: Engagement ~78, Participation ~74, eNPS ~60
- Undisclosed: Engagement ~53, Participation ~29, eNPS ~0 (negligible)

Female employees show the highest participation rate; male employees show the highest engagement score and eNPS. The undisclosed group is notably low across all metrics, likely reflecting a small subgroup.
