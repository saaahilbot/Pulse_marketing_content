Title: "Category – Key Metrics"
Ten engagement categories measured across Engagement Score, Participants Percentage, and e_NPS:
Alignment, Autonomy, Compensation and Benefits, Engagement, Personal Growth, Recognition, Relationship with Manager, Relationship with Peers, Wellness, Work Environment.

Notable patterns:
- Wellness scores the highest Engagement Score (~65) — employees rate their personal wellbeing well.
- Compensation and Benefits has the lowest e_NPS (~21) — a clear pain point for the organisation.
- Recognition and Personal Growth have notably low eNPS scores (~40–43), highlighting two high-priority areas for improvement.
- Relationship with Peers and Relationship with Manager are mid-range, suggesting interpersonal dynamics are stable but not exceptional.
