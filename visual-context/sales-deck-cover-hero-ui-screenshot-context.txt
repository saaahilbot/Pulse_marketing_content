2. VISUAL VIBE

Bold and energetic. Dark navy background, white and orange typography, a vibrant real-person photo. The product UI cards are clean, light, and instantly comprehensible — time estimates and question counts make them feel user-friendly and low-friction. The geometric diamond shapes add dynamic, contemporary visual energy. The smiling, enthusiastic person grounds the tech-forward slide in human positivity. It's a strong brand statement: serious about data, genuinely excited about people.

3. HOW THIS SUPPORTS "PROFESSIONAL YET PUNCHY" BRAND VOICE

The headline "Listening to Your Employees Powers Better Decisions" is the brand philosophy in one sentence — no fluff, no jargon, direct causality. The combination of a real person (warmth, relatability) + product UI cards (credibility, specificity) + dark premium background (authority) hits all three dimensions of "professional yet punchy." The time/question counts on the survey cards are a subtle but powerful detail: they communicate that Vantage Pulse respects both employees' time and HR leaders' need for rich data.
