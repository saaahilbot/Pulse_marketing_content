2. VISUAL VIBE

Emotionally charged and cinematic. White background, three greyscale portrait photos in rounded-corner cards, orange arrows driving the sequence forward. The greyscale treatment of the photos gives the slide a sense of urgency and weight — like a case study in slow-motion failure. The orange arrows inject energy and forward momentum. Minimal text per panel keeps reading friction low. The pull quote is the sharpest line in the deck.

---
3. HOW THIS SUPPORTS "PROFESSIONAL YET PUNCHY" BRAND VOICE

This visual does exactly what great marketing should: it makes the cost of inaction feel personal and immediate. Each stage has a first-person quote that puts words in the prospect's employees' mouths — making the abstract (turnover cost) concrete and human. The orange arrows are brand-consistent and visually direct. The pull quote — "Inaction isn't neutral, it's expensive" — is the brand voice distilled to a single sentence: professional authority wrapped in a punchy, uncomfortable truth. Ideal for email campaigns, sales outreach, or as a transition slide before the product demo.
