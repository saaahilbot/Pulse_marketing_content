1. CORE DATA / STAT SHOWN

AccessOne's employee NPS results measured via Vantage Pulse:
- Central eNPS Score: 45 (shown in a circular dial/donut indicator)
- Promoters: 58% (shown in teal/green with a happy face emoji)
- Passives: 29% (shown in amber/yellow with a neutral face emoji)
- Detractors: 13% (shown in red/pink with a sad face emoji)

An eNPS of 45 is considered a strong score (industry average typically 10–30), indicating significantly more promoters than detractors.

2. VISUAL VIBE

Data-meets-emotion. The three sentiment categories use colour-coded face emojis (happy/neutral/sad) that feel approachable and human, avoiding the sterility of traditional data viz. The large circular dial in the centre draws the eye first to the headline number (45), then the viewer naturally reads the percentage breakdown to the right. Dark navy background with white text creates high contrast and a premium feel. The layout is split: methodology text on the left, the visual metrics display on the right, with a thoughtful man's photo as a visual metaphor for the "thinking employee."
